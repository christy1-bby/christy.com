Artifical intelligence

Artifical Intelligence (AL)is the branch of computer science that focuses on creating machine to perform tasks that typically require human intelligence, such as reasoning, learning, and problem-solving. AI systems can analyze data, recognize patterns, and make decisions based on the information they process. They are used in various applications, including natural language processing, computer vision, robotics, and more

AI has the potential to revolutionize industries by automating processes, improving efficiency, and enabling new capabilities.
